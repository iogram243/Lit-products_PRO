# Author Today Context

Author Today is a writing and reading platform where authors manage books, series, publications, and community-facing materials. For Digital Shelf, treat it as a platform where users usually want helpers for author-side management and repeated publication routines.

## Core areas that matter for requests

- author profile and author-facing sections;
- book and series management workflows;
- publication-related metadata and repeated editing tasks;
- blog or community-facing materials where relevant;
- navigation between author sections and repeated routine operations.

## Publicly visible author-side signals and patterns

- Author Today public pages and support materials clearly indicate author-facing publication and commercial workflows;
- support and rules pages imply ongoing work with publications, blog records, comments, and user-facing materials;
- blog presence and community interaction are relevant enough to be considered in helper requests;
- authors likely care about maintaining books and related materials with less routine friction.

## Typical repetitive work an author may want to automate

- repeated metadata entry for books or related publication fields;
- switching faster between author sections and management pages;
- preparing or reusing text fragments for books and updates;
- structuring routine book-management actions into one helper flow;
- reducing repetitive manual work in publication and maintenance scenarios.

## Good wording patterns for AI-generated ТЗ

When the user writes about Author Today, the AI should often translate the request into developer language such as:

- helper for faster work with Author Today book management;
- tool for repeated metadata and publication actions;
- assistant for navigation between author sections and related materials;
- utility for maintaining books, series, and repeated author-side entries;
- extension or mini-tool that reduces routine actions in Author Today workflows.

## Frequent user intentions the AI should recognize

- "нужно удобнее управлять книгами" -> management helper for repeated edit flows;
- "нужно быстрее вносить данные" -> metadata/autofill/template helper;
- "хочу всё видеть компактно" -> dashboard or control panel for repeated author sections;
- "нужен помощник по публикации" -> publication workflow helper with structured repeated actions;
- "надо меньше рутины" -> automation of recurring author-side maintenance steps.

## Good scope examples for Author Today requests

- helper for repeated book-management actions;
- tool for structured entry of recurring metadata fields;
- assistant for navigation between book, series, and author-facing sections;
- utility for recurring publication maintenance and content updates;
- compact author workflow panel for repeated routine operations.

## Bad scope patterns the AI should avoid

- claiming secret internal moderation or billing access;
- inventing exact hidden controls not described by the user;
- turning a simple convenience request into a giant multi-system solution;
- pretending to know exact unstable UI labels if they were not given.

## Desired output behavior for Author Today

When producing ТЗ for Author Today:

- frame the task as author-side convenience, navigation, repeated entry, or publication maintenance;
- keep the request grounded in realistic writer workflows;
- preserve the user’s concrete problem and avoid speculative overengineering;
- use clear, practical language suitable for implementation planning.

## Example transformations

Raw user idea:
"Нужно удобнее управлять книгами на Author Today"

Better AI direction:
"Что нужно сделать:
Сделать инструмент для Author Today, который упрощает управление книгами и сокращает количество ручных действий при повседневной работе автора.

Желательно:
Добавить быстрый переход между основными разделами, связанными с книгами, сериями и повторяющимися действиями автора."

Raw user idea:
"Хочу чтобы быстрее все заполнялось"

Better AI direction:
"Что нужно сделать:
Сделать helper для Author Today, который ускоряет заполнение повторяющихся полей и помогает использовать заранее подготовленные текстовые заготовки при работе с публикациями.

Желательно:
Добавить хранение шаблонов для часто используемых описаний, служебных заметок и повторяющихся блоков текста."

## Constraints

- Do not claim exact hidden controls unless the user described them directly.
- Prefer neutral wording such as "в кабинете автора", "на странице книги", "в разделе управления книгой", "в публикационных формах".
- If the request is broad, assume the user wants convenience, speed, and fewer repeated edits rather than speculative complex integrations.

## Useful source direction

This context is based on public Author Today pages, support materials, rules, and public blog-related observations used only as conservative author-workflow guidance.
