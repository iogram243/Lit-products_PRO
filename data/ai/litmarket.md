# Litmarket Context

Litmarket is a marketplace-oriented fiction platform. For Digital Shelf, treat Litmarket as a service where the user often needs helpers for author-side publishing, editing, storefront preparation, and promo workflows.

## Core areas that matter for requests

- author page and book presentation;
- book cards, titles, descriptions, and visual materials;
- marketplace/store related content preparation;
- promotional and loyalty-related mechanics mentioned in public Litmarket materials;
- co-author or collaboration-oriented routines;
- blog/content support and repeated author-side form work.

## Publicly visible author-side signals and patterns

- Litmarket public materials emphasize marketplace logic, offers, promotions, and audience conversion;
- loyalty / bonus mechanics are publicly mentioned and matter for author-side promotion thinking;
- blog and communication content exist and can be part of promotion support;
- authors often need reusable content blocks and faster preparation of card-like materials.

## Typical repetitive work an author may want to automate

- filling repeated fields for cards, descriptions, and listings;
- preparing batches of promo text, graphics references, or reusable fragments;
- speeding up editing of marketplace-facing materials;
- navigating between repeated author-side content tasks;
- structuring tags, text blocks, and metadata with fewer manual steps.

## Good wording patterns for AI-generated ТЗ

When the user writes about Litmarket, the AI should often translate the request into developer language such as:

- tool for faster preparation of Litmarket book cards and descriptions;
- helper for repeated actions in marketplace-related author forms;
- assistant for promotions, loyalty scenarios, or repeated listing edits;
- utility for managing repeated text fragments, tags, and visual blocks;
- dashboard helper for daily work with author materials on Litmarket.

## Frequent user intentions the AI should recognize

- "мне нужно быстрее публиковать / оформлять" -> helper for repeated listing fields and reusable blocks;
- "нужна программа для продаж / акций" -> support tool for promo preparation, bonus/loyalty scenarios, campaign organization;
- "хочу меньше ручной возни с карточками" -> autofill, templates, preset fragments, batch preparation;
- "хочу собрать всё в одной панели" -> compact dashboard for routine author actions;
- "нужно красивее и быстрее оформлять материалы" -> helper for structured descriptions, promo blocks, and assets planning.

## Good scope examples for Litmarket requests

- tool for repeated preparation of Litmarket card descriptions and metadata;
- helper for assembling promo text and reusable fragments for sales campaigns;
- extension that groups daily author actions for faster workflow;
- assistant for bonus / loyalty-related content preparation without claiming direct control over platform internals;
- utility for managing repeated tags, descriptions, and visual references.

## Bad scope patterns the AI should avoid

- promising direct access to private finance or hidden marketplace logic;
- inventing exact monetization controls not described by the user;
- turning every sales-related request into a full analytics suite unless explicitly requested;
- assuming undocumented bulk-edit capabilities inside the platform.

## Desired output behavior for Litmarket

When producing ТЗ for Litmarket:

- lean into workflow speed, repeated listing actions, storefront preparation, and promotion logic;
- keep the request grounded in practical author tasks;
- convert vague "для продаж" wording into concrete author-facing helper actions;
- prefer tools that save time in repeated preparation and editing.

Better AI direction:

Сделать инструмент для Litmarket, который ускоряет оформление карточек книг и других авторских материалов за счет повторного использования готовых текстовых и визуальных заготовок.

Желательно:
Добавить быстрый выбор сохраненных шаблонов для описаний, тегов и повторяющихся блоков текста."

Raw user idea:
"Нужна штука для акций и бонусов чтобы было удобнее"

Better AI direction:
"Что нужно сделать:
Сделать helper для Litmarket, который помогает автору быстрее готовить материалы для акций, бонусов и промо-кампаний без ручной сборки каждого блока заново.

Добавить отдельные заготовки для акционных описаний, промо-текста и повторяющихся маркетинговых блоков."

## Constraints

- Do not invent exact internal buttons or payment mechanics unless the user described them.
- Prefer neutral wording such as "в кабинете автора", "в карточке книги", "в форме публикации", "в блоках продвижения", "в разделе оформления".
- If the user wants convenience, assume they mean faster preparation of materials, fewer repeated edits, and clearer workflow structure.

## Useful source direction

This context is based on Litmarket public descriptions, terms, and public-facing materials about marketplace, loyalty, and author-side work scenarios.
